ECO-FEST

Acts

Eco-Fest will host a number of acts throughout the day which will represent a number of different genres and entertain the whole family. Playing across two stages, acts will each perform for aproximately two hours each with activities to do throughout the day for the whole family. A free 3 hour child care session will also be taking place during the day so parents can enjoy the music while their children take part in numerous fun eco-friendly activities.
